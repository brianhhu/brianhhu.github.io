I am a Technical Leader at \href{https://www.kitware.com/ai/}{Kitware, Inc.} My research interests lie at the intersection of Computer Vision
and Natural Language Processing, with a current focus on AI Test and Evaluation (T\&E) and AI Alignment. I completed my PhD in Biomedical Engineering
from Johns Hopkins University, with a thesis on biologically-inspired artificial intelligence. I have served as the Principal Investigator or
Chief Scientist on numerous Department of Defense (DoD)-funded programs, leading teams of AI researchers and engineers to tackle complex problems.
